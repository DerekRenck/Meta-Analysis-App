Meta-Analysis App: Easy Stats Online

This website is designed to make meta-analysis accessible and easy for anyone, especially those who may have limited access to specialized statistical software or are new to meta-analysis. Its goal is to provide a user-friendly, web-based tool that allows researchers, students, and healthcare professionals to perform essential meta-analysis tasks without needing advanced desktop programs.

I first learned about meta-analysis through Dr. Randerson Cardoso and the Meta Analysis Academy. Inspired by this, I developed this app to help users explore meta-analysis online. I encourage users to try this tool while continuing to learn the R programming language and deepen their understanding of statistical programming.

Try the live app here: https://meta-analysis-app.shinyapps.io/meta-analysis-app/

More features will be added over time to enhance the functionality and usability of this app. Stay tuned for updates!
